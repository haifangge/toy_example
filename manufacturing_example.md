* Cover page
    * Broker name and logo
    * Broker contact details
    * Broking submission for ABC manufacturing Limited
    * Date of submission
    * Renewal date

* Risk overview

    Introductory couple of paragraphs explaning how this is a well managed risk, long established and long time partner of the broker, why the risk is being marketed. Including the following contents: 

    * Business description and activities
    * Date business establised
    * ERN number
    * Holding insurer
    * Target premium
    * Renewal date

* Products and covers required

    Section which lists the covers required, including the following contents: 

    * Property damage
    * Business interruption
    * Goods in transit
    * Specified all risks
    * Terrorism
    * Employers liability
    * Public and products liability
    * Directors and officers

    Often this will list out the limits, e.g., PD - All Risks, EL at £10m LOI, PL/Pr at £5m LOI etc. 

* Risk management

    This will tell a story of the management of the business, including the contents like: 
    
    * The business has a full-time risk manager
    * The business has ISO certifications
    * Commitment to environmental management
    * Long-standing contracts with major UK auto suppliers

* Property damage cover
    * Location details
        * Address
        * Contruction type
        * Year built
        * Floor area
        * Occupancy
    * Fire protection
        * Sprinker system
        * Smoke detection system
        * Fire extinguishers
    * Security
        * Monitored intruder alarm
        * CCTV 
        * Fencing and gated access
    
    * Sums insured
        * Buildings
        * Machinery and plant
        * Stock (Raw materials)
        * Stock (Finished goods)
        * Business interruption (Gross profit)

    * Loss history (past 5 years)

    * Risk management and improvements: such as rire risk assessment, staff fire safety training, preventive maintenance, and waste management etc. 

* Liability covers
    * Employers liability: This is a description of the different types of activities that the employees undertake, the risk management in place (e.g., H&S, guarding on machinery etc.), including a breakdown of the different types of activity and the associated wageroll, e.g., clerical, manual work away, manufacturing, sales etc. 

    * Public and and products liability: This is a description of the nature of the products, and the typical end customers, plus any quality standards the product complies with; where goods or parts are sourced from; any H&S relating to control aof access to sites Turnover breakdown by territory: UK, EEA, US and Rest of world. 

    * Claims: Listing and details of any EL and PL/Pr claims in the last 5 years.
